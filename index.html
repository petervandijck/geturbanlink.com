Urban Link makes city data easily accessible to everyone, for every city in the world.

We are building an API and easy to use browser for all the data in very city in the world. Think Google Analytics, but for cities. Neighborhood activists, city governments, businesses, academics, hackers - anyone will be able to use this data to build better cities. You could build an app that uses city mobility data, for every city in the world. You could use the data to pressure your government to adopt better policies. You could analyze the data to determine what works best for which types of cities.

Cities generate infinite amounts of data. Our hypothesis is that, if we can make that data freely and easily accessible by everyone, we (humanity) can build better cities.
